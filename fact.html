The Betelgeuse supernova


Betelgeuse, a red supergiant star located 725 lightyears away from our solar system is entering it's most exciting years. Any moment now(Sometime within the next 100,000 years), the star is expected to turn into a supernova, creating beautiful red flashes across our skies that could be seen from earth for at least two months.
Why did I bring this up? Everyone has their supernova moment. We just have to wait it out. Like Betelgeuse did for 10 million years.
